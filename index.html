About Us

Vendfun provides hospitality kiosks for your ease

Founded in 2020, Vendfun offers multi-functional hospitality kiosks for hotels and homestays. Our flagship product, the Vendfun Hybrid Kiosk, launched in Malaysia in early 2021. It combines self check-in/check-out and walk-in booking with vending capabilities, allowing guests to purchase snacks, drinks, and toiletries. Conceived by our founder and CEO, Benny Wee, during the COVID-19 pandemic, this innovative kiosk not only enhances guest convenience but also generates additional revenue for hotels. Designed to improve the guest experience from check-in to check-out, our kiosks ensure customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.
